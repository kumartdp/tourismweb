Pakhal Lake

Weather :   31° C

Timings : 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Time Required : 1-2 hrs

Entry Fee : No entry fee

Around 50 km away from Warangal city is the beautiful Pakhal Lake which is spread over an area of 30 sq km. Set amidst the lush green forests and hills, the human-made lake forms a highly picturesque location.The lake also borders a wildlife sanctuary located at its shore.
